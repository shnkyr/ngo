About Our Organization


Path of Sunshine is a non-profit organization committed to creating positive change in the world. Through various initiatives and programs, we aim to bring hope and happiness to underprivileged communities. Join us on our journey to make a lasting impact!

We are dedicated to fostering sustainable development, empowering marginalized individuals, and igniting a sense of purpose within communities. Our initiatives span education, healthcare, women's empowerment, and environmental stewardship, all with a shared goal of leaving a lasting, positive footprint on the world. Rooted in values of compassion, collaboration, and transparency, Path of Sunshine is a beacon of optimism that invites you to embark on a journey of meaningful impact. Join us in our quest to radiate warmth, inspire change, and sow the seeds of a brighter future.


Our Projects!


Education for Underprivileged Children

The "Education for Underprivileged Children" project is a core initiative of our NGO aimed at providing quality education and holistic development opportunities to children from marginalized and economically disadvantaged backgrounds. Our goal is to break the cycle of poverty and empower these children with the tools they need to create a brighter future for themselves and their communities.

    Project Objectives:
  1. Access to Quality Education
  2. Holistic Development
  3. Empowerment and Confidence
  4. Breaking Gender Barriers

Community Sanitation and Hygiene

The "Community Sanitation and Hygiene" project is a vital initiative led by our NGO to improve the overall health and well-being of underserved communities through the promotion of proper sanitation practices and hygiene education. We recognize that access to clean water, proper sanitation facilities, and knowledge about hygiene are fundamental rights that contribute to healthier lives and thriving communities.

    Project Objectives:
  1. Improved Sanitation Facilities
  2. Hygiene Education
  3. Behavioral Change
  4. Reduce Water Borne Diseases

Support for Elderly Citizens

The "Support for Elderly Citizens" project is a compassionate endeavor by our NGO to provide essential care, companionship, and resources to elderly citizens who often find themselves living alone or without proper support. We recognize the invaluable contributions of our senior community members and are committed to ensuring their dignity, well-being, and quality of life during their golden years.

    Project Objectives:
  1. Companionship and Emotional Support
  2. Healthcare and Basic Needs
  3. Dignity and Respect
  4. Empowerment
